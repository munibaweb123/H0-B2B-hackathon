An AI-powered multi-tenant B2B SaaS platform that gives Pakistani real estate agencies a single platform to manage property listings, clients, and sales pipelines — with AI property recommendations and automated WhatsApp/email follow-ups.

Problem We Are Solving
Pakistan has 50,000+ real estate agencies. They all manage clients through WhatsApp groups, Excel sheets, and paper diaries. No affordable, locally-tailored CRM exists. PropFlow AI fills this gap.
